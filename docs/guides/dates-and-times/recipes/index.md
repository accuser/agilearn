# Recipes: Dates and times

Short, task-focused how-tos for specific things you'll want to do with dates and times. Each recipe assumes you already know the basics — if a term surprises you, the [Learn](../learn/) section is one click away.

Unlike the tutorials, the recipes don't have a recommended order. Pick whichever is useful right now.

## Recipes in this guide

- **[Parse a messy date column](parse-a-messy-date-column.ipynb)** — strategies for data where dates arrive in several different formats.
- **[Compute durations and ages](compute-durations-and-ages.ipynb)** — days between dates, age in years, "human" durations like "3 days ago".
- **[Convert between time zones](convert-between-time-zones.ipynb)** — turning UTC into local time, handling DST transitions, and round-trips.
- **[Avoid common date/time mistakes](avoid-common-datetime-mistakes.md)** — the naive/aware mix, DST gaps, the January 1st gotcha, and others.
